I like to reimagine live interactions in 4 panel comic strips.

* Sound effects added for exaggerated (re)action: BANG, POOF, SPLAT, YEAH! 
* Empty pauses for the expressions that don't land, or when self-awareness settles in
* One-liners that had no pun intended but I cannot help to read between the lines
* Internal monologue for all forbidden outputs

Must've been all the Garfield read on toilets as a kid and [Dilbert Comics](https://screenrant.com/funniest-dilbert-comics-scott-adams/) I shockingly found out still applies.
